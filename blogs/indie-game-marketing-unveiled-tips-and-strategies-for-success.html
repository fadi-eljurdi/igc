Indie Game Marketing Unveiled: Tips and Strategies for Success

Your Ultimate Guide for Proper Indie Game Marketing

Lots of game developers tend to invest a copious amount of time and effort into
conceptualizing all the way to developing high-quality games. The level of effort put into
a game can vary widely based on an array of factors such as the team’s expertise and
experience, the game’s scope and genre, which platforms it will feature, and the target
audience. It is known for a fact that game development is a fast-paced and highly
competent field that requires numerous complementary functions, that include, creating
a compelling storyline, coding, testing, promotion, among others.

Even though as we have just stated above that indie game development is a
multifaceted process that requires various tasks, we will be focusing on the marketing
side of things and how to market and showcase your end-product… In other simpler
words, “Indie Game Development Marketing 101!”

The Bread and Butter of Marketing

When thinking of marketing, what is the first thing that pops into your mind? You
guessed it, social media. Well, I am quite sorry to burst your bubble, but there are much
more important things that are essential. Don’t get me wrong! Social media is as equally
important as the other means of promotion, but in a complementary sense; which
means that social media marketing alone is not enough. What is the key to unlock this
riddle then I hear you ask? Keep on reading to find out.

App Store Optimization

We have all heard of search engine optimization (SEO), but never heard of app store
optimization (AOC).

When it comes to mobile game development, 50% of applications are discovered
through stores’ search function. The name of your game should be optimized according
to specific keywords that are relevant to your game’s genre. Additionally, a good copy
goes a long way to how you present your game and highlight why the consumer should
download it. Furthermore, the way you communicate your game’s message is also a
crucial factor to your success.

Message Boards

One effective marketing technique for indie game developers out there would be
posting your game on popular message boards such as Reddit, IGN, Steam Discussions,
etc.

Message boards are still popular in 2023 and a viable non-invasive option as they still
have many visitors and active members that start threads on various topics including
gaming trends and development. Moreover, you could get real feedback and get your
game critiqued which would contribute to a more successful and tailored experience.

Press Releases

Press releases are announcements sent to journalists or bloggers to inform them about
any breaking news or information about a particular subject; could be about your
business for instance or about your new game you just released.

Participate in Events

There are dozens of online and physical indie video game events all over the globe such
as AfricaComicade Gamathon, Game Devs of Color Expo 2023, Google Play’s Indie
Game Festival, and many others. What’s exciting is you could get to meet your target
audience in-person and use a direct sales strategy through selling or even giving away
physical copies of your game if it’s available for pc.

The Power of Influencers

We can’t deny the fact that people love to see what their favorite youtuber is up to and
are willing to try whatever he does. Gaming channels and influencers on youtube and
Twitch could be the perfect candidates for product endorsements and advertising. Such
a promotion could dramatically increase downloads and sales of a certain game due to
the simple fact that their favorite influencer is playing it.

Leveraging Content Marketing

Written content is an often overlooked step in a comprehensive marketing strategy for
indie games. Blogs, email newsletters, and other forms of written materials for instance
could help you in building awareness for your brand and a loyal fan base. Content
marketing takes consistency and patience, in addition to crafting content that would
appeal to your target audience. Incorporating teasers and screenshots of your newly
developed game for instance could trigger curiosity in your followers, which increases
the probability for visitors to take action on your website or landing page such as
subscribing, downloading, or purchasing.
Utilization of Social Media

Now comes everybody’s favorite part, social media marketing. No one can deny the fact
that social media is here to stay. Not only is it thriving and having its users increase
exponentially, but it has become one of the most important channels for modern day
advertising. How can it specifically help indie game developers? They could exploit
social media ads for a very thorough targeting strategy to reach an audience that would
be interested in your product, creating a mighty and efficient approach that would be
highly successful in getting potential clients to take action.

To recap, a properly developed game without effective marketing would not let your
game reach its full potential and get the appreciation it deserves. It might even render
your efforts futile. By following the above steps however, rest assured, that the time and
sweat you spent will not go in vain.
